Quick Reference Cards

Essential reference information for quick decision-making on the field. Click any card to expand and see detailed information.

Restarts

How to restart play

Free Kick: Direct or Indirect
Penalty Kick: 12 yards from goal
Throw-in: From touchline
Goal Kick: From 6-yard box
Corner Kick: From corner arc
Dropped Ball: Neutral restart

Cards

When to show cards

Yellow Card:
  • • Unsporting behavior
  • • Dissent
  • • Persistent infringement
  • • Delaying restart
Red Card:
  • • Serious foul play
  • • Violent conduct
  • • Denying goal opportunity
  • • Offensive language

Offside

Offside basics

Offside Position:
  • • Closer to goal than ball
  • • Closer to goal than 2nd last opponent
  • • In opponent's half
Involved in Play:
  • • Interfering with play
  • • Interfering with opponent
  • • Gaining advantage

Handball

Handball decisions

Handball Offense:
  • • Deliberate hand/arm contact
  • • Hand/arm above shoulder
  • • Hand/arm makes body bigger
  • • Hand/arm in unnatural position
Not Handball:
  • • Ball hits hand/arm directly
  • • Hand/arm close to body
  • • Supporting body in fall

Equipment

Required equipment

Required:
  • • Shirt with sleeves
  • • Shorts
  • • Socks
  • • Shinguards
  • • Footwear
Prohibited:
  • • Jewelry
  • • Dangerous equipment
  • • Electronic devices

Signals

Referee signals

Common Signals:
  • • Direct free kick: Point forward
  • • Indirect free kick: Arm raised
  • • Goal: Point to center
  • • Corner kick: Point to corner
  • • Goal kick: Point to 6-yard box
  • • Advantage: Both arms forward
